About Miniature Coin Art

  • Originated in Rajasthan and Mughal courts centuries ago.
  • Created using brushes finer than a strand of hair.
  • Each coin artwork takes 2–3 days of intricate work.
  • Made using eco-friendly paints and archival coatings.
  • MudrArt revives this rare tradition through modern technology.
